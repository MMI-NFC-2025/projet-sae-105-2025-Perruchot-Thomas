About this Project

I am Thomas Perruchot, a student at the MMI department in Montbéliard. This site was created for an exercise on "Mobile First" design.

I chose the Matilda Effect because it is at the intersection of information design and social justice. Nettie Stevens' story is not an anecdote; it is the symptom of a patriarchal system that actively erased its women.

This site is a modest attempt to use our digital tools to participate in repairing this oblivion.
