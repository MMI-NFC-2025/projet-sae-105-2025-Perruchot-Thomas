Glossary

Matilda Effect
Theorized by historian Margaret W. Rossiter, this term designates the systematic denial or minimization of female scientists' contribution to research, their work often being attributed to their male colleagues.
Patriarchy
A social system where authority and power are primarily held by men. It generates structural biases (in science, politics, family) that lead to the invisibilization and devaluation of women.
Chromosome
A microscopic element present in our cells, carrying genetic information. Nettie Stevens discovered the role of sex chromosomes (X and Y).
Systemic (or Structural)
Designates a problem that is not due to isolated individual actions, but is integrated into the very functioning of a system, organization, or society.
