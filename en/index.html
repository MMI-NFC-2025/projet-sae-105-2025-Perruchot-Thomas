Nettie
Out of Focus

Oblivion is not an accident.

The Matilda Effect is a systemic mechanism: it refers to the denial or minimization of women scientists' discoveries. This site explores this patriarchal bias through the story of Nettie Stevens, and how culture is repairing this erasure today.
